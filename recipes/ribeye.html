Ribeye Recipe

Description

Simple juicy ribeye steak cooked in the oven, grill or even air fryer, packed with flavour without needing a lot of fancy ingredients

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak
  • Black pepper
  • Butter
  • Whatever seasoned salt you have

Steps

Two methods: Grill and Oven

Grill method

  1. Bring Steaks to room temperature and season per the recipe below
  2. Heat grill to medium heat. Season steaks and grill between 5 to 7 minutes on each side depending on rare to well-done
  3. Remove steaks, dot them with butter, and allow to rest about 5 to 10 min covered in aluminum foil before serving

Oven method

  1. Preheat the oven and cast iron skillet or ovenproof pan per the recipe below
  2. Sear the seasoned ribeye steaks for two minutes per side. Once browned on both sides, place in pan in oven and roast according to instructions below
  3. Remove pan and place cooked steaks on a plate and add butter pats to each piece. Make a foil tent and let them rest for 5 to 10 min. (Important to keep them nice and juicy)
