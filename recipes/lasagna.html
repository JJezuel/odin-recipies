Lasagna Recipe

Description

A lasagna recipe with meat sauce as the base. Layer sauce with noodles and cheese and bake until bubbly. Great for a big family

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • half medium onion, diced
  • half large bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can good quality tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
  • quarter cup chopped fresh parsley, packed
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder or garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon to quarter cup sugar
  • Salt
  • half pound dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1.5 pounds mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • quarter pound freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, and garlic; and back the beef
  4. Make the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Preheat oven to 375 F
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Bake
  9. Cool and serve
