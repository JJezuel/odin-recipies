Omelette Recipe

Description

A simple, yet delicious base omelette recipe which you can then add your own fillings freely. Add cheese, ham, herbs, mushrooms, or even onions for a healthy and diverse breakfast/lunch.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tsp butter

Steps

  1. Beat eggs
  2. Season beaten eggs with salt and pepper
  3. Heat the oil and butter in a non-stick frying pan over medium-low heat and butter is melted and foaming
  4. Pour eggs into the pan
  5. Tilt pan to allow eggs to swirl and cover pan surface
  6. Let mixture cook for 20 seconds
  7. Scrape line through middle with spatula
  8. Tilt pan to fill surface back with runny egg
  9. After one or two times and egg has set, fill omelette with what you like
  10. Fold gently in half with spatula
  11. Slide onto plate to serve
